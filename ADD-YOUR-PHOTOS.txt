====================================================
  SARORA JEWELRY — HOW TO ADD YOUR PRODUCT PHOTOS
====================================================

Save your photos into the  images/  folder next to index.html,
using these exact filenames:

  images/hero.jpg          → The lifestyle rings-stacking photo (white top, two hands)
                             OR the tray flat-lay — this is the big hero background

  images/ring-wave.jpg     → The double wavy ring on finger (close-up)

  images/ring-signet.jpg   → The bold angular/crossover signet ring

  images/rings-stack.jpg   → The stacking lifestyle shot (two rings, two fingers)

  images/bracelet.jpg      → The adjustable Byzantine chain bracelet (hanging on pine)

  images/bracelet-clasp.jpg→ The SARORA clasp close-up (on grey stone)

  images/pendant-moon.jpg  → The crescent moon pendant (worn on black top)

  images/moon-closeup.jpg  → The dark background moon + signet dish shot

  images/flatlay.jpg       → The ceramic tray flat-lay (all pieces together, light bg)

  images/flatlay-dark.jpg  → The dark navy background flat-lay

  images/logo.png          → The Sarora logo (white background with spiral mark)

TIPS:
- Any standard image format works: .jpg .jpeg .png .webp
- Aim for square or portrait-oriented photos for the gallery tiles
- Hero/product cards look best with portrait photos (taller than wide)
- The website shows elegant placeholders wherever a photo is missing,
  so you can add photos one at a time

After saving the photos, just open index.html in your browser —
no server needed, it works as a local file!
